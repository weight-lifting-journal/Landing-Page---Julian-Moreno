Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my body weight?

Weigh yourself and take a picture of the scale.

How much?

Free with ads. $10.99 per month without ads.

How do I edit a routine?

Click on the routine tab. Select what you would like to change and input the info.

I miss some logs in calendar. How show them all?

To see all logs for day select the desired date in calendar and click the "logs" button above it. This will show you all data for that day.

How can I support app development?

If you like the app please write a review on the App Store. This will help us and other users a lot.
